Hello! My name is Erin Ballinger, and I found a passion for web development in early high school. I found my interest in the interactive and entertainment side of the web. When my friends and I played a lot of the Jackbox Games series, party games that use one computer for the game with the players interacting through the jackbox website, I always had in the back of my mind "How did they do that?" and "How can I do that?"

My interest in these party games led me to my path of web design, both front and back end. In high school and recently in college through my Input Stack project, I have made multiplayer projects utalizing Google's realtime database. Through my course work, I have been constantly evolving as a web dev, making more professional and corporate sites, along with my own projects I have designed, developed, and shipped. Links to these projects and more in-depth explanation can be found on the experience page.

Currently, I am a freshman (class of 2027) attending Indiana University Indianapolis. I am pursuing double B.S. in Informatics and a B.S. in Media Arts and Science with a specialization in Web Development at IUI's Luddy Indianapolis school. My future career goal is to be a Full Stack Developer.

In my free time, I develop my personal sites (most of which are hosted on my GitHub), play music (right now im learning ukelele), along with developing games, 3d modeling, and coding C# scripts for solo unity projects.
